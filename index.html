Hi — I'm Miriam

Hi! I'm Miriam - a licensed hairstylist and certified extension specialist with 6 years behind the chair. I absolutely adore what I do. To me, hair isn't just about a color or a cut — it's about connection. From the first consult to the final style, I cherish every moment spent with my clients. It's in those conversations, laughs, and relationships that the magic really happens.

Hair has the power to transform more than just how we look — it transforms how we feel. That's why I pour my heart into every appointment. My chair is a space of trust, beauty, and self-love.

Giving back is a huge part of my mission. Every year, I offer free haircuts for families during the holidays, and I'm working toward expanding that outreach - especially for kids in the foster care system. Every child deserves to feel special, valued, and confident. My dream is to open a salon that's more than just a salon — a community space where beauty meets purpose, and everyone leaves feeling seen, celebrated, and cared for.

Let's change the world, one haircut at a time.
